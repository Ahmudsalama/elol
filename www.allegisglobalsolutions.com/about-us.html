Workforce Experts Built to Unleash Human Impact

To enterprise is human. At Allegis Global Solutions (AGS), our “why” lies in pursuing goals that sit beyond the horizon. It’s a mindset that requires being audaciously bold, committing to a vision others can’t see but will redefine what’s possible. To enterprise means inventing solutions that move us forward and turn strengths into an unstoppable force for change.

We’re here to advance missions. Meeting clients where they are, we design a plan and guide them along a transformational journey, applying bold actions and diverse minds to solve the most complex challenges – from permanent and extended workforce management to services procurement, consulting, direct sourcing and our Universal Workforce Model™. Because we believe that when you build a workforce that’s designed to harness human enterprise, you design a workforce that’s built for impact.

AGS Quick Facts

  • Privately held
  • Operating company of $14.5 billion Allegis Group
  • Headquartered in Hanover, MD
  • 4,000+ employees globally
  • Supporting customers in 100+ countries worldwide
  • 3,000+ supplier relationships

A History Steeped in Growth

Our consultative approach to workforce transformation began decades ago. As we continue to elevate the industry, we’ll continue to push the boundaries to unlock the potential of tomorrow’s workforce.

  • 1983: Parent company Allegis Group founded
  • 2001: Established Allegis Group Services
  • 2002: First US program live
  • 2007: First program in Europe
  • 2010: Expanded to APAC
  • 2012: First LATAM partnership
  • 2014: Rebranded to Allegis Global Solutions
  • 2016: Introduced Acumen
  • 2019: Launched Procurement Solutions
  • 2020: Recognized as a Beeline Certified Strategic Partner
  • 2021: Announced strategic partnerships with Brightfield and Globality
  • 2022: Released the Universal Workforce Model
  • 2023: Introduced “The Human Enterprise” tagline

Industry Recognition

We’re proud to be a company of people who don’t just go to work – but who are passionate and mission-driven to deliver customer value. This focus has earned AGS recognition as a visionary thought leader and best-in-class global workforce solutions provider from industry leading organizations, including Everest Group, Staffing Industry Analysts (SIA) and HRO Today.

Why We Love Working at AGS

Working at AGS is more than just a job. It’s a career. It’s a community of people who invest in your development and empower you to blaze your own trail. We also represent over 100 countries and speak dozens of languages. So as you’re building relationships and doing your job, you’ll be exposed to other cultures and advancement opportunities while expanding your knowledge of global markets and strategies.
