AGS Leader Named to Staffing Industry Analysts’ 2023 Diversity, Equity and Inclusion Influencer List

HANOVER, Md., May 18, 2023 – Allegis Global Solutions (AGS), the leading provider of global workforce solutions, is pleased to announce that Quinne Teske, the global director of diversity, equity and inclusion (DEI) and employee experience at AGS, was recently recognized on Staffing Industry Analysts’ (SIA) 2023 DEI Influencers – US and Canada list. 

 

Teske has redefined the DEI strategy at AGS by fostering an environment of equity, inclusion and transparency; gaining executive buy-in; engaging leaders across multiple departments; and embedding DEI in executive decision-making processes. She has spearheaded key initiatives, including partnering with talent acquisition and HR teams to implement diversity hiring panels, piloting the Mentor U initiative to provide equitable opportunities for underrepresented talent and ensuring the entire DEI team was certified as inclusive coaches.

 

“Quinne has been an effective and committed DEI leader for the entire AGS team,” said Vice President of People Strategy Sara Babin. “Her initiatives have given leaders across the globe a greater understanding and insight into the significance of driving outcomes for hiring, retaining and advancing high-potential diverse talent, and setting standards for inclusive behaviors.”

 

Under Teske’s leadership, AGS has built robust marketplace DEI capabilities, including inclusive branding, talent diversity, supplier diversity and advisory services, that help our clients build a diverse workforce and foster an equitable and inclusive environment where everyone feels they belong and can succeed.

 

“Quinne’s leadership, mentorship and influence have not only impacted our internal DEI journey, but also the journeys of our clients and partners,” added Babin. “Her actions have evolved the approach to DEI from ‘the right thing to do’ to a business imperative – helping uncover opportunities for our clients to unleash the potential in their greatest assets – their people.”

 

SIA’s annual list honors people who have made an impact in DEI within the workforce ecosystem. “In the wake of economic uncertainty, organizations’ commitment to DE&I efforts can give way to other business priorities. But those truly devoted to DE&I have not slowed down or deprioritized this vital effort,” said SIA Chief Operating Officer Ursula Williams. “SIA honors those making the world of work a place where all can belong, and where all can succeed. We are very proud to see many of those faithful advocates grace our 2023 DE&I Influencers list."

 

To learn more about AGS’ Marketplace DEI Solutions, please visit https://www.allegisglobalsolutions.com/en-au/capabilities/diversity-inclusion.
