Four AGS Executives Named to SIA’s Staffing 100 for 2022

Hanover, Md., February 18, 2021 – Allegis Global Solutions (AGS), the leading provider of global workforce solutions, proudly announces four of its senior leaders being named to Staffing Industry Analysts’ (SIA) North America Staffing 100 list for 2022. 

Now in its 11th year, SIA’s Staffing 100 honors industry leaders who are charting a course into the future of workforce solutions. The following group of leaders were recognized for their impact on AGS and the industry over the past year:

  • Maria Boyse – Vice President of Business Development
  • Chad Lane – President
  • Bruce Morton – Managing Director of Global Strategy
  • Steven Schumacher – Senior Vice President of Global Operations and Business Development

“The last two years have been transformative for our industry, but AGS emerged from the setbacks of 2020 even stronger than before. They demonstrated resilience and commitment to excellence while continuously evolving to meet our customers’ dynamic needs,” said Allegis Group President Andy Hilger. “I’m proud to see this group of leaders recognized by SIA for their deep industry knowledge and influence on the future of workforce solutions.”

All four leaders have been previously recognized in SIA’s Staffing 100: Boyse (2019); Lane (2017, 2018, 2019, 2020, 2021); Morton (2020); and Schumacher (2017, 2020, 2021). 

For more information on Allegis Global Solutions and its industry-leading workforce solutions, visit AllegisGlobalSolutions.com or follow @AllegisGlobal on Twitter or @Allegis-Global-Solutions on LinkedIn.
